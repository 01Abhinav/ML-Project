Academic Performance Predictor
Predict a student's mathematics score with other known features.

The following inputs are required for predicting required result

  1. Gender of Student
  2. Race/Ethinicity of Student
  3. Parental Level of Education
  4. Lunch time type
  5. Test preperation course status
  6. Reading Score
  7. Writing Score

Following are the regressor models trained and validated for the best.

  1. Random Forest
  2. Decision Tree
  3. Gradient Boosting
  4. Linear Regression
  5. XGBRegressor
  6. CatBoosting Regressor
  7. AdaBoost Regressor
